About Me

The Story Behind
the Lens

"I don't just record moments — I craft them into stories that people feel long after the screen goes dark."

I'm Siddharudha M B, a passionate videographer and video editor from Karnataka. Since 2021, I've been turning ordinary moments into cinematic stories — from intimate wedding films to electric event highlight reels.

Currently pursuing Computer Science Engineering at Basaveshwar Engineering College, I bring a unique blend of technical precision and artistic vision to every project. As Media Head for IEEE BEC Student Branch, I lead all visual storytelling — shaping how our chapter is seen by the world.

Through my brand sidvlogs.in, I specialize in cinematic storytelling, documentary-style visuals, corporate content, and same-day social media reels — delivering quality without compromise, always on time.
